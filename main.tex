\textbf{Theorem (The Cauchy-Schwarz Inequality)}\\
Suppose $\vec{x},\vec{y} \in \mathbb{R}^n$. Then 
\begin{equation}
|\vec{x} \bullet \vec{y}| \le \|\vec{x}\| \|\vec{y}\| \label{cs-ineq}.
\end{equation}
